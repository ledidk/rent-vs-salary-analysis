## 📌 Project Overview
In this project, I analyzed rental prices across major Canadian cities and compared them with junior data analyst salaries.

The goal was to understand how affordable rent is for someone starting a career in data analytics.

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- In cities like Vancouver and Toronto, rent takes a large portion of income  
- Even in Ottawa, rent is close to or above the recommended 33% threshold  
- This highlights affordability challenges for entry-level professionals
